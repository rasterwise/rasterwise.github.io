SEO Audit Screenshots

Capture SERP results, track ranking positions visually, document on-page SEO elements, and create compelling client reports with automated screenshots.

Get Started - From $5/mo Try Live Demo

The Challenge

SEO professionals need visual evidence of rankings, on-page elements, and progress over time. Manually capturing screenshots for audits and client reports is time-consuming and inconsistent.

  • Manual SERP screenshots are tedious
  • Client reports need visual proof
  • Hard to track visual changes over time

The Solution

GetScreenshot automates SEO visual documentation. Capture SERPs, page layouts, meta elements, and more. Use keyword highlighting to call attention to specific mentions.

  • Automated SERP screenshots
  • Keyword highlighting for visual emphasis
  • Full page capture for complete audits

Example: Weekly Ranking Report

Automated SERP capture with keyword highlighting

  1. 1

    Trigger: Weekly Schedule

    Set up a weekly trigger in Zapier to capture ranking positions every Monday.

  2. 2

    Action: GetScreenshot with Keyword Highlight

    Capture the SERP with the client's brand or target keyword highlighted.

    url=https://google.com/search?q=target+keyword
    highlight=ClientBrand
    width=1440
    fullpage=true
  3. 3

    Action: Save to Client Folder

    Store screenshots in date-organized folders in Google Drive or Dropbox.

  4. 4

    Action: Add to Report

    Automatically append to Google Slides or Notion for client presentations.
