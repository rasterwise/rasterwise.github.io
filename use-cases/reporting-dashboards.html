Use Case

Dashboard & Report Screenshots

Automate dashboard captures and generate visual reports. Share analytics snapshots via email, Slack, or embed them in documents without manual screenshots.

Get Started - From $5/mo Try Live Demo

The Challenge

Sharing analytics and dashboard data often requires tedious manual screenshots. Teams waste time capturing, cropping, and distributing visuals when they could be analyzing data instead.

  • Manual screenshots are time-consuming
  • Dashboards require login access to view
  • Stakeholders want regular visual updates

The Solution

GetScreenshot automates dashboard capture and distribution. Schedule regular screenshots of your analytics tools, send them via email to stakeholders, or post them to Slack - all without manual intervention.

  • Scheduled automated captures
  • Direct email delivery to stakeholders
  • PDF reports for presentations

Popular Dashboard Workflows

Automate these common reporting tasks with GetScreenshot

Daily Metrics Email

Capture your Google Analytics or Mixpanel dashboard every morning and email the screenshot to your team.

Weekly Slack Reports

Post weekly dashboard snapshots to your #metrics Slack channel automatically every Monday morning.

Client Reporting

Generate PDF reports of campaign dashboards to send to clients without giving them direct dashboard access.

Executive Summaries

Capture high-level KPI dashboards for executive briefings and board presentations.

Historical Snapshots

Archive dashboard states at regular intervals to track changes and build historical records.

Alert Notifications

When metrics trigger an alert, capture and send the relevant dashboard view as part of the notification.
